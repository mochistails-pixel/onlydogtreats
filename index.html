Our Story

I'm a dog owner, dog trainer, and dog walker—so dogs are my LIFE, especially my own pup, Mochi.

I find it frustrating when looking for delicious, high-quality, and affordable dog treats that everything always has some weird ingredients in it. I know it's not good for them, whether it's about overall health, dental health, or unnecessary grains.

I wanted to create a single ingredient dog treat brand so that no matter which product you buy, you get what you see—just one single ingredient per product. It’s ONLY the best for our dogs. I plan to launch in 2026 if all goes well and hope to make a ton of dogs happy with delicious, clean treats!
