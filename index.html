Our Story

I'm a dog owner, dog trainer, and dog walker—so dogs are my LIFE, especially my own pup Mochi.

I find it frustrating when looking for delicious, high-quality and affordable dog treats that everything always has some weird ingredients in it where I know it's not good for the dog, whether it's about overall health or dental health. Grains, whatnot.

So, I wanted to create a single ingredient dog treat brand so that no matter which product you buy, you get what you see—just one single ingredient per product. It's only the best for our dogs. I plan to launch in 2026 if all goes well and hope to make a ton of dogs happy with delicious clean treats!
