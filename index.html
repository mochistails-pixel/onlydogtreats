Our Story

I'm a dog owner, dog trainer, and dog walker... so dogs are my LIFE, especially my own pup, Mochi.

Like many dog owners, I find it incredibly frustrating to find treats that checked all the boxes: delicious, high-quality, and affordable. It seems like every bag I pick up is full of 'weird' ingredients, grains, and fillers that I know aren’t good for my dog’s overall or dental health.

That’s why I want to create a brand where transparency is the only ingredient. My philosophy is simple: one product, ONLY one ingredient. No fillers, no surprises; just the best for our dogs. I'm working hard to bring these clean, delicious treats to you in 2026!
